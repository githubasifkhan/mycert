PROPERTY MANAGEMENT

Property Management is the operation, control, maintenance, and oversight of real estate and physical property. This can include residential, commercial and real estate. This Module provides an extensive feature for managing all the real estate properties and the contracts in an efficient manner.

Features

  • Property Contract Management

  • Agreement Contract Management.

  • Contract and Agreement closure.

  • Dynamic Reports.

HOW TO USE THIS MODULE

Installing, this module will provide with menus such as Property, Contract, Reports and Configuration. The first menu is property which will invoke the property master.

In Property Master property details can be specified.

Floor Master include details related to the property selected.

Unit Master include details for each unit with respect to the floor and property. Unit Type, Unit Sub type, Unit View, Unit Usage are the master files which needs to be configured.

Generate Floors will create more than one floors , based on the floor count for the property specified.

Similarly, Generate Units will automatically create more than one units for the property specified.

Property Agreement is used for landlords. When the property is selected, the related units will come in the Units section. Total Rent amount is required.

When you click on rent button , the rent amount will come in the rent Tab and the in the installments column you need to specify the month duration.

By clicking on the payment button, the rent amount gets splitted into 6 installments.

In the costing tab, the amount gets divided into 12 months.

Amount in the payment and costing entries can be posted individually

Agreement can be closed by choosing the closure menu. When you click on process button, the entries get generated in the Account Settlement Tab.

The Agreement can be closed/ Completed or Terminated by clicking on the check box terminate.

Similar to Agreement, we can customer Contract. Here in customer contract you need to mention the unit rent, accordingly by clicking on Rent button, you will get rent details. Also the rent installments month duration to be considered.

By clicking on Payment in the receipts tab you can see the rent is divided into 6 installments.

In the revenue tab you can see the rent is splitted into 12 months. Each entries in the revenue and receipts can be posted individually.

Customer contract can be closed by clicking on the closure menu. When clicked on process, it will generate entries. Contracts can be terminated or completed.
